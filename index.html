Mission

Our mission is to promote women in sports, teach the sport of rowing, and develop student-athletes to their highest potential in a safe and positive environment while encouraging discipline, commitment, and integrity both on and off the water.

Facilities

The Boathouse at Bladensburg Waterfront Park

Our boathouse has been home to our lovely shells and early morning practices since the beginning. Located on the scenic Anacostia river, the Bladensburg boathouse is homebase not just for our team, but also Maryland Men’s Crew, Catholic University, DeMatha High School, and even some Masters! It’s always fun seeing each other on the water practicing to be the best we can be.

Address: 4601 Annapolis Road, Bladensburg, MD 20710

Directions from Baltimore Ave South Near Campus: Left onto US-1ALT S, continue straight onto Annapolis Road, turn right into the Park immediately after getting on Annapolis Road.

Cole Fieldhouse Erg Room

We share our erg room with Maryland Men’s Crew. It’s where we have our evening practices everyday and morning practices when the weather is not in our favor. The ergs are what we use for strength and endurance training in order to prepare for upcoming races.

Address: 2126 Campus Dr, College Park, MD, 20740

Directions: Located in the Cole Student Activities Building (next to Stamp Student Union) right next to the UMD Bike Shop. The entrance is on the side of the building.
